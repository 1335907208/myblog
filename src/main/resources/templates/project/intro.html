QUEENS PEAK

NOT JUST A RESIDENCE BUT A STATEMENT OF WHO YOU ARE

Rising forty-four storeys into the sky across two residential towers, Queens Peaks' sinuously contoured balconies give the facade a sculptural presence that at once evoke a sense of awe.

Impeccably positioned in the Queenstown district, the new lifestyle hub with the added bonus of a city fringe location and MRT connectivity, Queens Peak is truly close to everything that you have ever wished for.
